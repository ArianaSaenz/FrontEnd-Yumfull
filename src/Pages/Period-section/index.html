Frequently asked questions

1. Can I eat chocolate during my period?

Yes, but in moderation. Prefer dark chocolate without sugar.

2. Is it good to drink tea while menstruating?

Yes! Herbal teas like chamomile can ease cramps and bloating.

3. Should I avoid dairy products?

Only if they worsen your symptoms like bloating or acne.
